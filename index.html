Now is a great time to drink beer!

Beer has been around since the beginning of civilization. It has survived the test of time and become integral to many cultures. In the aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic breweries have faced a difficult time recovering.The goal of this project is to highlight the different kinds of beers offered in each state! Navigate around and discover a brewery near you!

Data Overview

The dataset is provided on Kaggle by Jean-NicholasHould. Inside we find 2,000+ canned beers from 500+ breweries in the United States. The main addition to this dataset is the coordinates of the breweries. Using Python alongside Geopy and Nominatim I was able to find all coordinates for each of the breweries. Some false positives were corrected (e.g. did not produce a coodinate within the US) leading to this dataset containing beers, breweries, and their locations.

Findings

The most popular beer is the American IPA and Colorado is the state with the most breweries, 47 of them! The beer scene is sparse in Nevada and West Virginia, possibly due to local laws. A large amount of breweries appear to be condensed where New York and New Jersey connect, potentially a great area to hit many places during a weekend.
